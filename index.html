About Me

Hello! I recently graduated with an MSc in Digital Marketing from Queen Mary University of London and am a CFA Level I candidate.

I am passionate about data analytics, visualisation, and machine learning, transforming complex datasets into clear, actionable insights. I focus on combining marketing, finance, and analytical expertise to deliver practical solutions and drive meaningful outcomes.

This portfolio showcases my projects, skills, and hands-on experience, demonstrating the value I can bring to graduate programmes and entry-level roles in analytics, finance, and consulting.

Beyond data and analytics, I enjoy working with start-ups, experimenting with innovative digital strategies, and building projects that turn ideas into tangible results.

I am always eager to learn, collaborate, and take on new challenges. If you have an exciting project or opportunity, I would be delighted to connect and explore how we can work together.
